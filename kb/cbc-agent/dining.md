# Dining at Coral Beach & Tennis Club

## Overview
CBC offers exceptional dining experiences across multiple venues, featuring fresh seafood, local Bermudian produce, and international cuisine. Each venue offers a unique atmosphere while maintaining the club's high culinary standards.

## Dining Venues

### Main Dining Room
**Style**: Elegant, formal dining  
**Setting**: Ocean-view dining room with panoramic Atlantic vistas  
**Service**: Breakfast, lunch, and dinner  
**Atmosphere**: Sophisticated yet welcoming, perfect for special occasions  
**Specialties**: Fresh seafood, seasonal menus, fine wines  
**Dress Code**: Smart casual to elegant  

### Beach Terrace  
**Style**: Casual outdoor dining  
**Setting**: Oceanfront terrace with direct beach views  
**Service**: Lunch and dinner, weather permitting  
**Atmosphere**: Relaxed, breezy, quintessentially Bermudian  
**Specialties**: Grilled seafood, tropical salads, refreshing cocktails  
**Perfect for**: Casual meals, sunset dining  

### Members' Bar & Lounge
**Style**: Classic Bermudian bar and lounge  
**Setting**: Traditional club atmosphere with comfortable seating  
**Service**: Cocktails, light fare, appetizers  
**Atmosphere**: Intimate, social, perfect for conversation  
**Specialties**: Craft cocktails, premium spirits, bar snacks  
**Features**: Classic bar games, comfortable seating areas  

### Beach Bar
**Style**: Casual sand-side service  
**Setting**: Directly on the beach with pink sand floors  
**Service**: Drinks, light snacks, beach fare  
**Atmosphere**: Fun, tropical, relaxed  
**Specialties**: Frozen cocktails, beer, beach snacks  
**Perfect for**: Beach days, poolside relaxation  

### Snug Room
**Style**: Private dining and meetings  
**Setting**: Intimate private space  
**Service**: Custom menus available  
**Atmosphere**: Exclusive, quiet, personal  
**Capacity**: Small groups, private parties  
**Use**: Business meetings, private celebrations
