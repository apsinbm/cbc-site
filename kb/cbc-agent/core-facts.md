# Coral Beach & Tennis Club - Core Facts & Brand

## Overview
Coral Beach & Tennis Club (CBC) is Bermuda's premier private members' club, located on 26 acres of pristine South Shore coastline in Paget Parish. Established in the 1940s, CBC represents the pinnacle of Bermudian hospitality and exclusivity.

## Location & Setting
- **Address**: South Shore, Paget Parish, Bermuda
- **Size**: 26 acres of oceanfront property
- **Beach**: 2,000 feet of world-famous pink sand beach
- **Views**: Panoramic Atlantic Ocean vistas

## Key Features
- Private pink sand beach with 2,000 feet of coastline
- Multiple dining venues with ocean views
- Professional tennis and pickleball courts
- Championship croquet lawn
- Modern fitness center and spa facilities
- Elegant wedding and event venues
- Luxury accommodations (cottages, suites, guest rooms)

## Membership & Access
- **Membership**: By invitation only
- **Guests**: Welcome with member approval
- **Philosophy**: Exclusivity balanced with warm Bermudian hospitality
- **Character**: International membership maintaining authentic Bermudian charm
