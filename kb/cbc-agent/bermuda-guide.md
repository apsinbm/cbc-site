# Bermuda Basics & Transportation Guide

## About Bermuda

### Geography & Location
- **Location**: British Overseas Territory in the North Atlantic Ocean
- **Distance**: 650 miles east of North Carolina, USA  
- **Size**: 21 square miles (54 square kilometers)
- **Population**: Approximately 64,000 residents
- **Capital**: Hamilton
- **Historic Town**: St. George's (UNESCO World Heritage Site)

### Government & Currency
- **Status**: British Overseas Territory with internal self-government
- **Currency**: Bermuda Dollar (BMD), pegged 1:1 with US Dollar
- **Accepted Currency**: US Dollars widely accepted
- **Language**: English (official)
- **Time Zone**: Atlantic Standard Time (AST)

## Famous Features

### Natural Attractions
- **Pink Sand Beaches**: Unique pink-hued sand from crushed coral and shells
- **Crystal Caves**: Underground limestone caves with crystal formations
- **Coral Reefs**: Some of the northernmost coral reefs in the world
- **Turquoise Waters**: Stunning blue-green ocean colors
- **Spiny Lobster**: Local delicacy, different from Maine lobster

### Cultural Highlights
- **Bermuda Shorts**: Traditional business attire, worn with knee socks and blazer
- **Gombey Dancers**: Traditional colorful folkloric performances
- **Codfish Breakfast**: Traditional Sunday breakfast with codfish cakes
- **Dark & Stormy**: Bermuda's national drink (Gosling's rum and ginger beer)
- **Architecture**: Distinctive white limestone roofs and pastel colors

## Transportation in Bermuda

### Important Transportation Rules
- **No Car Rentals**: Private car rentals not available to tourists
- **Size Restrictions**: Only small vehicles allowed on roads
- **Alternative Transport**: Taxis, buses, ferries, scooters, walking, cycling

### Taxi Services

#### Availability & Costs
- **Widely Available**: Throughout the island, at hotels, attractions
- **Expensive**: Among the most expensive taxi rates globally
- **Metered Service**: All licensed taxis use meters
- **Tipping**: 15-20% customary
- **Large Groups**: Mini-vans available for groups

#### Booking Taxis
- **Hotel Concierge**: Most reliable method for reservations
- **Taxi Stands**: Available at major hotels and attractions
- **Phone Booking**: Can be arranged by concierge
- **Airport**: Always available at L.F. Wade International Airport

### Scooter & Moped Rentals

#### Requirements
- **Minimum Age**: 16 years old with valid driver's license
- **Helmet Required**: Mandatory for all riders by law
- **Insurance**: Included with rental, additional coverage available
- **Experience**: No prior scooter experience required, brief instruction provided

#### Rental Details
- **Daily Rates**: Approximately $50-80 per day
- **Weekly Rates**: Discounts available for longer rentals
- **Fuel**: Rental includes full tank, return with fuel
- **Locations**: Multiple rental locations across the island
- **Safety**: Bermuda has narrow, winding roads - drive carefully

### Public Transportation

#### Ferry Service
- **Routes**: Connects Hamilton, Royal Naval Dockyard, St. George's
- **Scenic**: Beautiful harbor and ocean views during trips
- **Frequency**: Regular service, seasonal schedule variations
- **Cost**: Affordable option for longer distances
- **Bicycles**: Many ferries accommodate bicycles

#### Bus Service
- **Extensive Network**: Covers most of the island
- **Pink & Blue Buses**: Distinctive color-coded route system
- **Exact Change**: Exact fare required, no change given
- **Bus Passes**: Day, week, and month passes available
- **Schedule**: Regular service, reduced frequency evenings/weekends

### Cycling

#### Bike Rentals
- **Widely Available**: Hotels and rental shops throughout island
- **Daily/Weekly Rates**: Reasonable pricing for short and long term
- **Electric Bikes**: Available for easier hill climbing
- **Safety Gear**: Helmets recommended, lights for evening riding

#### Cycling Conditions
- **Hilly Terrain**: Bermuda has many hills, moderate fitness required
- **Narrow Roads**: Share roads with scooters and buses
- **Railway Trail**: 18-mile recreational trail, perfect for cycling
- **Scenic Routes**: Beautiful coastal roads and inland paths

## Transportation Tips for CBC Guests

### Airport Transfers
- **Pre-Arranged**: CBC can arrange airport pickup/drop-off
- **Reliable Service**: Professional drivers familiar with club
- **Luggage Assistance**: Full service from airport to accommodation
- **Flight Monitoring**: Pickup times adjusted for flight delays

### Getting Around from CBC
- **Taxi Service**: Most convenient for dining and shopping
- **Scooter Rental**: Best for exploring at your own pace
- **Ferry to Hamilton**: Scenic trip to capital city
- **Organized Tours**: CBC concierge can arrange guided excursions

### Cost-Saving Tips
- **Combine Trips**: Group activities to minimize taxi costs
- **Public Transport**: Use ferries and buses for sightseeing
- **Walking**: Many attractions within walking distance of each other
- **Rental Packages**: Multi-day scooter rentals more economical

## Safety & Practical Information

### General Safety
- **Very Safe**: Bermuda has low crime rates
- **Traffic**: Drive on the left side of the road (British system)
- **Speed Limits**: Maximum 35 mph (56 km/h) anywhere on island
- **Emergency**: Dial 911 for police, fire, or medical emergencies

### Practical Tips
- **Dress Code**: Smart casual generally appropriate
- **Bermuda Shorts**: Acceptable business and formal wear for men
- **Sun Protection**: Strong sun reflection off white roofs and pink sand
- **Reef Safe Sunscreen**: Protect Bermuda's coral reefs with eco-friendly products
- **Duty Free Shopping**: Take advantage of no sales tax
- **Electrical**: 110V/60Hz (same as US and Canada)
