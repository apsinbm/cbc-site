# Activities & Experiences at CBC

## Beach Activities
CBC's 2,000 feet of pink sand beach offers endless opportunities for relaxation and adventure.

### Water Sports
- **Snorkeling**: Explore Bermuda's coral reefs and marine life
- **Paddle Boarding**: Calm water stand-up paddleboarding
- **Kayaking**: Single and tandem kayaks available
- **Swimming**: Safe, protected beach areas
- **Beach Games**: Volleyball, frisbee, beach tennis

### Beach Relaxation
- **Beach Lounging**: Premium beach chairs and umbrellas
- **Beach Cabanas**: Private beachside retreats (bookable)
- **Beach Reading**: Quiet spots for peaceful reading
- **Sunset Watching**: Spectacular Atlantic sunsets
- **Beach Walks**: Miles of pristine coastline to explore

## Social Activities

### Evening Entertainment
- **Cocktail Evenings**: Regular social mixers in the Members' Bar
- **Live Music Nights**: Local and visiting musicians
- **Wine Tastings**: Curated wine experiences with expert sommeliers
- **Dancing**: Social dancing events and lessons
- **Themed Parties**: Seasonal celebrations and special events

### Cultural Experiences
- **Guest Lectures**: Topics on Bermudian history, culture, and nature
- **Art Exhibitions**: Local artist showcases
- **Cultural Tours**: Bermuda heritage and historical sites
- **Photography Workshops**: Capture Bermuda's beauty
- **Cooking Classes**: Learn Bermudian cuisine techniques

## Excursions & Tours

- **Nature Tours**: Crystal Caves, botanical gardens, nature reserves
- **Shopping Excursions**: Hamilton and St. George's shopping districts
- **Golf Tours**: Access to Bermuda's championship golf courses
- **Sailing Adventures**: Boat trips and sailing experiences

### Cultural Immersion
- **Local Markets**: Traditional Bermudian markets and artisans
- **Culinary Tours**: Restaurant and local food experiences
- **Art Gallery Tours**: Bermuda's vibrant art scene
- **Maritime Heritage**: Shipwreck sites and maritime museums
- **Traditional Crafts**: Bermudian pottery, woodworking, and more
